Design Decisions:-
1. To Prevent Shift Reduce Conflist, the grammar of expressions was modified
2. The modified grammar of expressions
E ::= ~ E
    | inverse E
    | E .+. E
    | E .-. E
    | E .*. E
    | E ./. E
    | E + E
    | E - E
    | E * E
    | E / E
    | E % E
    | ! E
    | E && E
    | E || E
    | E = E
    | E <> E
    | E < E
    | E <= E
    | E > E
    | E >= E
    | intval
    | ratval
    | boolval
3. We will do type checking during evaluation of AST
